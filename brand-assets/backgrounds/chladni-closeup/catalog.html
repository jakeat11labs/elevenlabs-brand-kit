Chladni Close-Up Backgrounds 15 Files

Base image layers from the Brand Design System Figma file. These are landscape and nature photography used as the foundation for the Chladni Close-Up background treatment.

Compositing pipeline: These images are BASE LAYERS, not final backgrounds. In Remotion, composite them as follows:

1. Base image layer (this file) - full bleed, object-fit: cover
2. Apply blur - backdrop-filter: blur(80px) or filter: blur(80px)
3. Noise overlay - noise-texture.jpg with mix-blend-mode: overlay at opacity: 0.8
4. Optional: Chladni SVG curve overlay on top

Shared texture files: backgrounds/noise-texture.jpg and backgrounds/seamless-texture.jpg
